About

I write about tech, teaching, and my hometown. With a background in digital project management and technical, creative, and grant writing experience, I hope to bring something fresh to your next project.

2017-2018: National Endowment for the Humanities

  • White Paper, co-written and edited with instructional team
  • Educational Materials

As Senior Institute Assistant, I developed lesson materials, created and edited tutorials, and was a contributing writer and editor on the final white paper. I coordinated all writing and editing assignments for the team using Kanban project management.

2018: Storyburgh

  • Drag is for Everybody

As a student writer, I was tasked with writing about a community in Pittsburgh. As soon as I met Princess Jafar, it was clear her she was the creative I needed to share. This piece also appears in the fall 2018 issue of Steel This Magazine.

2018: Groupie style guide

  • Groupie house style guide

When a friend of mine announced she was starting a music blog, I asked if I could write a style guide for her and her writers. I took on the task so I could get some experience with editorial styling. After researching a few other style guides, including an old one from VICE magazine, I put together the beginnings of Groupie's style guide for writers. As the project gets off the ground, this guide is intended to keep non-traditional music writers on track— an anchor for DIY creatives just trying to express their visions.

2019: Command line fundamentals

  • Command line fundamentals: a command line workshop for humanists

My work during the 2017 NEH Institute taught me the basics of the UNIX command line, but it was more a trial-by-fire situation than a workshop one. As an instructor at the 2019 DH@Guelph annual Summer Workshops, I was able to create materials, teach, and troubleshoot four days worth of exciting command line materials.
